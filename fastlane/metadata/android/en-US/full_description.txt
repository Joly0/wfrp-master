Get rid of unwieldy paper character sheets! WFRP Master won't get in your way while you are busy fighting monsters, exploring world or just hanging in the tavern.

- Assemble your party and invite other players
- Manage your attributes, skills, spells, and more
- All your data are safely backed up in the cloud and synced to other party members in real time
- Tailored to 4th edition of Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay
