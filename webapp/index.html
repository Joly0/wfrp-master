WFRP Master

WFRP Master is Android app that aims to help you manage your Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay sessions. It replaces the paper character sheets with digital version which is backed up online and available for other players in your party. You can also plan your encounters and keep your NPCs in the app.

I hope this app helps you, so you can spend more time roleplaying instead of fighting with tools. Anyway this app gets new features on a regular basis, so let me know what you think!
